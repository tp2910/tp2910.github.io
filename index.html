Tavish Pattanayak

I am a 2nd year PhD in Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Tech , working as a graduate researcher in the ASDL (Aerospace System Design Lab) under Prof. Dimitri Mavris. My research interests include design optimization, aerodynamics and uncertainty quantification.

Previously, I graduated with a Masters in Information Technology (MSIT-PE) degree from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). During my time at CMU, I worked as a Research Assistant under Professor Hoda Heidari (CMU) and was also a Visiting Research Scholar at Syracuse University under Professor Ferdinando Fioretto during Summer 2022.

I'm incredibly passionate about mentoring aspiring, next-gen researchers in my field of interests (as it reminds me of the times I lacked guidance and resources on pursuing an academic career during my undergrad days)! Don't hesitate to reach out to me!
If you have any queries related to my work or a potential collaboration, please feel free to connect with me!
