Rakshit (Rocket) Naidu

I am an incoming PhD in Computer Science and Engineering student at Georgia Tech. I am a recipient of the Stewart Topper Fellowship for the academic year of 2023-24.
My research interests hover around Resposible ML and Trustworthy AI.

Previously, I graduated with a Masters in Information Technology (MSIT-PE) degree from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU).

I'm incredibly passionate about mentoring aspiring, next-gen researchers in my field of interests (as it reminds me of the times I lacked guidance and resources on pursuing an academic career during my undergrad days)! Don't hesitate to reach out to me!
If you have any queries related to my work or a potential collaboration, please feel free to connect with me!
