Tavish Pattanayak

I am a 3rd year PhD in Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Tech , working as a graduate researcher in the ASDL (Aerospace System Design Lab) under Prof. Dimitri Mavris. My research interests include design optimization, aerodynamics, human factors, and uncertainty quantification.

Currently, my research is on Uncertainty Quantification for the Electrified Propulsor Flight Demonstrator Program sponsored by NASA. Concurrently I am also exploring issues relating to human factors in aerospace. Previously, I graduated with a Masters in Aerospace Engineering degree from Georgia Tech, wherein I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant on Real-time Visualization of Simulation Results using Animation Tools for racecars sponsored by an automotive OEM. If you have any questions on my work, or if you think we can collaborate or just for general discussions, please do not hesitate to contact me!
